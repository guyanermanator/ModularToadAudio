Frequently Asked Questions

▶ What file formats should I send?
For mixing: send your individual tracks as WAV or AIFF, 24-bit or 32-bit float, at your session's sample rate (44.1 kHz, 48 kHz, etc.).
For mastering: send the final stereo mix as a WAV or AIFF, at least 24-bit, with 3–6 dB of headroom (peaks around −3 to −6 dBFS).
▶ How long does a project take?
Standard turnaround for a single track is 48–72 hours after all files are received. Larger projects are discussed individually. Rush turnarounds may be available — ask via the contact form.
▶ How does the new-customer discount work?
Simply mention that it's your first time booking in the contact form. The 50% discount applies to one track of mixing or mastering (your choice). No code needed — just reach out!
▶ What if I'm not happy with the result?
Your satisfaction matters. Revision rounds are included in every package — if something isn't right, we'll fix it. We work with you until you're happy with the result.
▶ How do I send my audio files?
You can attach files directly in the contact form (up to 25 MB). For larger sessions, send a Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer link in your message — we'll get back to you with next steps.
