About ModularToadAudio

Our Story

ModularToadAudio is a company focused on helping independent artists sound great at a good rate. We believe that professional-quality audio shouldn't be locked behind major-label budgets.

Born from a love of music and a fascination with the engineering that makes it come alive, ModularToadAudio bridges the gap between bedroom recordings and release-ready, broadcast-quality audio.

We work with musicians, podcasters, content creators and game developers — anyone who wants their audio to be the best it can possibly be.

Our Mission

"Creative Audio for Independent Artists & Developers."

Every project, regardless of size or budget, deserves a careful, creative approach. We bring professional-grade tools, trained ears and genuine enthusiasm to every track, episode and session that comes through the door.
