Our Story

Modular Toad Audio is a company with a passion for audio and other independent creatives. Your passion becomes our passion.

Music provides us with a soul-enriching escape that transcends language and society. I started this pursuit to do what I love and to help others do what they love too.

Since 2016, we have been a part of audio as a practice and the enjoyment of experimentation.

Like most creative pursuits, we persist because we choose to. I choose this. Let's get started.
