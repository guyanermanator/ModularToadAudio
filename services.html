Services

Every project is treated with care, whether it's a single track or a full album. Need something not listed here? Reach out and we'll put together a custom quote.

Mixing

A great mix is the foundation of a great record. We balance every element — levels, panning, EQ, compression, reverb and effects — so your music sounds cohesive, powerful and emotional on every speaker system.

Mastering

We add the final polish that makes your music competitive on Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube and beyond. Each master is crafted to translate well on headphones, club systems, and everything in between.

Loudness Normalization

Make sure your audio meets the loudness standards required by every major platform. We target the correct integrated LUFS, true-peak limits and dynamic range so your content is accepted everywhere without penalty.

Stem Preparation & Separation

Need your tracks broken down for remixing, sync licensing, or game audio integration? We export properly labelled, gain-staged stems. We also offer AI-assisted stem separation when you only have a stereo mix but need individual elements.

Custom & Larger Projects

Full albums, game soundtracks, commercial work, or bundles of services? We love working on larger projects and offer custom pricing to fit your needs. Get in touch and we'll put together a tailored quote.
